For whom is SoberSteps?

People in the process of overcoming addictions and returning to themselves.

  • Anyone whose phone is both trigger and support — we turn it into refuge
  • People in 3 AM crisis moments when the brain says "just one"
  • Those whose addiction is loyalty to damage, not weakness
  • Anyone who needs to celebrate small, invisible wins

Substance: Alcohol, marijuana, cocaine, heroin, crack, meth, opioids.
Behavioral: Gambling, sex/pornography, social media, shopping, video games, workaholism, self-hatred, perfectionism, toxic relationships.
