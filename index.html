A self-sustaining system. No starting point — enter from any side.

Smile

Not emotion — curiosity. Posture toward the unknown. Resistant to circumstances in a way joy isn't. I wonder what comes next.

Perspective

Replaces goal. Goal says where to arrive and ends. Perspective says how to look — and never ends. Always something you haven't seen yet.

Path

The context that holds it together. You walk not because you have a goal, but because the map is infinite. When one element weakens, two others sustain it.

Return to Self — heart of SoberSteps 2026

The most original module. No other recovery app has this. Three elements: Smile (curiosity, not emotion) — Perspective (how to look, never ends) — Path (infinite map). Symmetric: enter from any side.

🔍

Diagnostic

10 questions (A/B/C/D). Score 0–30. Four diagnoses: Returning to Yourself, The Inner Critic, The Invisible Wound, Survival Mode. Personalized first step + 30-Day Path.

🏃

30-Day Path

Week 1: Awareness. Week 2: Distance. Week 3: Repair. Week 4: Integration. Each day: Practice (2–5 min) + Reflection + Reminder. 3-sec pause before entry. One day per 24h.

🪞

Mirror Moment

Days 7, 14, 21, 28. Fullscreen front camera (mirror-flip), 3 min timer, mantra. Day 21: unlock celebration.

📋

Streak — days without self-flagellation

7-point daily checklist. Not days without error — days you were gentler with yourself. Color indicators: green ≥100%, blue ≥75%, gold ≥50%, orange ≥25%, red below.

📓

80% is enough journal

"I love X in myself despite Y" + "One small action for myself today (my 20%)." After 7 entries: automatic insight — recurring themes.

💭

Karma Mirror

Evening question: "Did I do myself as much good as I would do someone I love?" Timeline with dates.

💬

Naomi Mode

4 rotating questions about your motivational person (from profile). Claude API: 2–3 sentence warm, personalized feedback.

🧱

Wall of Strength

Global anonymous feed. One sentence per week after completing Path. "I survived this week and that's enough." Auto moderation.

Day 30: celebration animation + Patryk's voice (TTS): "Congratulations, brother. You returned to yourself." Smile ↔ Perspective ↔ Path enabled for good.

For whom is SoberSteps?

People overcoming addictions and returning to themselves.

Substances: Alcohol, marijuana, cocaine, heroin, crack, meth, opioids.
Behavioral: Gambling, sex/pornography, social media, shopping, video games, workaholism.
Return to Self: Self-hatred (free), Perfectionism (PRO), Toxic relationships (PRO).

Patryk AI

The real goal behind SoberSteps.

PatrykAI is an assistant built from a real person who went through homelessness, addiction, loneliness, distant fatherhood. Not a product — an extension. A voice that understands pain from the inside.

Three apps fund it: SoberSteps (start here), DadLine (long-distance parenting), MirrorMind (synchronicity journal). All on Smile ↔ Perspective ↔ Path. Revenue goes directly to building PatrykAI.

This isn't a business plan. It's the Path.
